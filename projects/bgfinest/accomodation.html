Best Luxury Hotels in Belgrade


ART HOTEL


This Art-hotel has its own restaurant, which is located on the stunning pedestrian street and shopping area of Belgrade. It offers air-conditioned rooms with a minibar, free Wi-Fi, free sauna and hairdryer. Restaurant-bar Mosaic, which is located on the territory of the Art-hotel overlooking the Knez Mihailova walking street, offers a wide selection of wines and various specialties that will appeal to everyone. Meals are made from fresh seasonal products, you can also try a variety of coffees and homemade desserts that will appeal to small guests. The hotel provides 2 conference rooms with multimedia equipment. These rooms have large windows, special individually controlled air conditioning, and sliding doors with a terrace overlooking the old part of the city. Attractions are located near the Hotel. You can easily get to: Belgrade Fortress and Kalemegdan Park, and you can also visit the historic quarter of Belgrade. Nikola Tesla Airport Belgrade is 12 miles from the hotel, so the Hotel can arrange airport transfers for a fee. You should visit Stari Grad if you like the nightlife of the city, good traditional food and the history of the city.



MOSKVA


One of the most significant architectural constructions of the Serbian capital is the Hotel Moskva, which was built in the style of Russian architecture and has been under the protection of the Russia since the second half of the last century. In 1908 the hotel was opened by King Peter I Karageorgievich. In addition to luxurious rooms and suites, a wonderful cafe and restaurant, the renovated hotel has a ballroom, meeting rooms, and a new spa center, which will please everyone. Hotel "Moscow" respects traditions and has the highest standards of modern professional service.



COURTYARD BY MARRIOTT


In the center of Belgrade is the Courtyard by Marriott Hotel. Guests can take advantage of the hotel’s services, such as: the international cuisine restaurant Courtyard by Marriott City Center with air conditioning and free internet. Rooms are equipped with comfortable and modern furniture, as well as in the rooms there are: flat-screen TV, work space, safe, cotton sheets, soft pillows, shower, toiletries, hairdryer. The hotel lobby provides guests with a bar, convenience store, business center and fitness center. Sights are located close enough to the hotel to easily visit them. Republic Square is 11 meters from Courtyard by Marriott Kiev City Center, to the pedestrian shopping area of Knez Mihailova Street - 121 meters. All shops and restaurants are within walking distance. The park and the fortress of Kalemegdan are located in 751 m from the Hotel. The nearest airport to the hotel is Nikola Tesla. The airport is 8.1 miles from the Hotel. We advise you to visit Stari Grad, the nightlife of the city will be visible there.



METROPOL PALACE


The Metropol Hotel has 2 restaurants. The rooms in this hotel are truly elegantly decorated. The hotel provides free Wi-Fi. Belgrade’s city center is just 400 meters from the Metropol. The hotel was being renovated in 2012. In the hotel you can use: spa center, swimming pool, sauna, hot tub, steam bath, gym. In-room amenities include an LCD satellite TV, air conditioning, safe, hairdryer. The reception is open 24 hours a day and has the ability to exchange currency as well as dry cleaning and room service. In the hotel you can find an ATM and several souvenir shops. In close proximity are the Houses of Parliament and the Church of St. Mark. The Metropol Palace Hotel is a 10-minute walk to Knez Mihailova Street with many shops where you can buy everything you need. The bus and train station is 1.5 km from the Hotel, and the A1 motorway exit is 2 km away. Nikola Tesla International Airport is 20 km away. To ensure a comfortable road to the Hotel or from the Hotel Metropol Palace offers airport transfer at an additional cost.
