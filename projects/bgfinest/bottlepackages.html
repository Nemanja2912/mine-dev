Bottle Packages

The Best VIP Bottle Service


Bottle services is your ultimate source for a true VIP experience. Whether you’re attending for a special celebration or simply want a night of great music and friendly socialization, you’ll no doubt want to splurge on a private table and bottle service. This service is considered a luxury in Belgrade that is worth experiencing during your visits to Belgrades venues. Bottle package is the easiest way to ensure you have the best experience possible.



If we are surely already interested in you, you want to know how much this pleasure will cost. Tables in the main rooms of the nightclubs typically run between 500€ and 1000€ per group (price of 3-6 premium bottles), depending on their position. Standard VIP table minimum is 250€ for 2 premium bottles. Bottle Packages are fully customizable and prices depend wich nightclub you choose.
